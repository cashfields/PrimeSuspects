Chapter 0260 Tapestry Thread


The Tapestry Threads

This is the concept of color threads for factors of seven (7) , they are separated by thirty (30). A color will peak out through the weave every 30 units , but will be on the seven (7) line. This chart is sorted by the PS or non-prime column.

Look at 7 x 7 . Then down thirty (30) to 7 x 37. Next , is 7 x 67 . Then is 7 x 97. And , missing n grows by seven (7) each time. From 1 to 8 to 15 to 22. Note that seven (7) times the first four primes are missing , because they are already sorted out. There is a block of eight colors , the light green repeats at 49 , 259 , 469 , 679 , 889 , . . .

The column *fam formula is the value that solves the equation PSL (PS -*fam) / 30 an integer.

Each of the eight *fam has its own color to repeat on the seven (7) line.
