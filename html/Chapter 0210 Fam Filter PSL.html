Chapter 0210 Fam Filter PSL

This is a short chapter. There are two sheets in the file “Tio Cash Master 0210 Fam Filter”. The first sheet “0210 prime Fam Filter PSL” will list the PSL of prime numbers for a *wildcard number and its two associated *fam.

The second sheet “0211 non-prime Fam Filter PSL” will list the PSL (really the MN) of nonprime numbers.

The sheets are protected and allow input to the *wildcard value and the *fam values. These filters will be used to generate the data for the next four chapters.
