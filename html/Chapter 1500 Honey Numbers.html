The *fam are listed in the first eight columns (f01 to f29). Look at column f01 , each row is 'just another 30 away'.

    a) *f01 / 1 = all the numbers divided by the base number of the *fam = ratio

I noticed in column "sq 19" some integer values , I reconized these as some of the PS (Prime Suspects).
But where were the rest of them ?
At the time *fam01 was not in the table , no point looking for a ratio of a number divided by 1.
So , I added data and columns for *fam01. The rest of the PS values showed up.
The unique thing is only in *fam01 and *fam19 could I see integer values.
There were no integer values in any of the other *fam (f07 , f11 , f13 , f17 , f23 , f29) ; only a decmial value could be found.
It is a littlr bit of an eye test , only looking in the blue painted columns are the square roots.
